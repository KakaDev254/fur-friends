Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our services, membership, or events? Check out our FAQ or reach out to us directly.

What are the membership plans available?

▼

We offer Starter, Community, and Family Packs designed to suit every pet family's needs. Each plan includes access to play areas, events, and exclusive perks.

Can I bring multiple pets?

▼

Absolutely! Our Family Pack is perfect for households with multiple pets. Each pet enjoys full access to our safe and fun spaces.

Are exotic pets allowed?

▼

Yes! We welcome dogs, cats, and exotic pets like birds, reptiles, and small mammals. Special care guidelines apply for their safety.

How do I join an event?

▼

Simply check our Upcoming Events section or contact us. Some events may require RSVP depending on space and activity type.
